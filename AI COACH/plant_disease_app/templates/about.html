About Plant Disease Detection

Empowering farmers and gardeners with AI-powered plant health monitoring

Our Mission

We believe that every plant deserves the best care possible. Our mission is to make advanced plant disease detection accessible to everyone, from home gardeners to large-scale farmers.

Using state-of-the-art deep learning technology, we've trained our AI on thousands of plant images to accurately identify 38+ different diseases across 14 plant species. Our goal is to help you catch diseases early and take action before they spread.

How It Works

1

Upload Image

Take a clear photo of the affected plant leaf or fruit and upload it to our system

2

AI Analysis

Our deep learning model analyzes the image and compares it to thousands of disease patterns

3

Get Results

Receive instant diagnosis with confidence score and detailed information about the disease

4

Take Action

Follow our recommended treatment and prevention measures to save your plants
